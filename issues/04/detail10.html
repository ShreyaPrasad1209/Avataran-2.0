Shreyas Joshi

Will you be my Valentine?

Fleeting glances, trembling lips
Pounding heart, a head that dips
A storm of thoughts, yet none to voice
I watch her so, let my heart rejoice
With mustered courage, I call her name
Take a step forward, suppressing my shame
She turns her head, a gentle wind blows
Our eyes meet, my cheeks emit a crimson glow
"Speak up. Yes, it's harder than making rhymes.
Now is your chance, stop wasting time."
Such were the thoughts, in my head that weighed
"Hi! There was something I wanted to say."
She gave a nod and thus I began
With wobbly knees and no game plan
"Like fireflies add charm to a moonless night
And new ideas deprecate a writer's plight"
"Like a silent sea longs for a raging river
And the Cupid now waits to pull an arrow from his quiver
Such is the way for your love I pine
Dearest of all, will you be my Valentine ?"
